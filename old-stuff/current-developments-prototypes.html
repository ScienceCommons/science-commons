2. Enhanced Visualization of Complex Designs

Enhanced visualization of effect sizes for more complex designs by displaying small (side-by-side) plots of original and replication data patterns on hover/mouseover (e.g., complex interaction pattern). Try it by hovering over the original or replication effect size cells (dotted underline).
